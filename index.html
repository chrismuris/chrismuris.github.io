I am an associate professor in the Department of Economics at McMaster University. My research is in the area of theoretical econometrics. I develop and study new tools for the analysis of panel (longitudinal) data. CV (pdf)

Information about my research is below, and at Google scholar and arXiv.

You can reach me at muerisc@mcmaster.ca.
